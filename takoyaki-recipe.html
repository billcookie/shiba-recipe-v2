Takoyaki

Ingredients

  • Octopus (500g)
  • Two large eggs
  • Regular flour
  • Pickled ginger
  • Green onions
  • Water (300ml)

Preparation

  1. First, siv the flour into a large mixing bowl and add water and the eggs.

  2. Keep mixing the mixture until it has no lumps and is fairly smooth.

  3. Next prepare your octopus, green onions and pickled ginger by cutting them into small pieces.

  4. Place into your takoyaki machine cooker and move using your skewer.

  5. Wait until golden brown before serving and then enjoy!
